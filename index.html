GRIDS

A Short Introduction

The grid has served as a compositional structure throughout the history of aesthetic culture and is ubiquitous in art, music, architecture, and design that we perceive everyday. The grid is a powerful compositional tool, and is also much more: it's an icon, an interface, and an ideology. The grid does not simply organize units in a matrix, it's a conceptual system for aesthetic content, which posits a certain way of seeing and understanding the world.
